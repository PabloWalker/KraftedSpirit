Method
Add Peels, & acids to a bowl and stir to combine. Let sit for at least one hour. Add Water to the bowl, stir and add to a blender. Blend for 10 seconds. Strain through fine mesh into a clean container. Juice the peeled fruit used and strain into the container, shake gently and store in the fridge for ~2 weeks.
